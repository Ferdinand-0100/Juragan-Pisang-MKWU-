Empowering Local UMKM

Juragan Pisang bridges the gap between dedicated local growers and the international market. We focus on elevating agricultural UMKM (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises) by providing them a direct platform to showcase their premium export-quality banana leaves.

By connecting buyers directly with the source, we ensure fairer prices for the farmers, fresher produce for the clients, and a sustainable, transparent supply chain.
